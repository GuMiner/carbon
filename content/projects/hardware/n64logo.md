![A Nintendo 64 Logo, in plastic.](/projects/hardware-projects/printing/n64-logo/n64.jpg "N64 Logo")

Available from [Thingiverse](http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:14519), this print was a test of a larger object and of a thin-walled part.

Print time was 180 minutes.

![The broken leg of the N64 logo.](/projects/hardware-projects/printing/n64-logo/n64_innards.jpg "N64 Broken Leg")

This print was performed with 5% infill, and with a very thin wall, leaving an extremely hollow interior.

Unfortunately, this infill ratio was insufficient. As a result, one of the legs partially broke, revealing the interior structure.
