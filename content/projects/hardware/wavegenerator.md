I spent some time in [Visio](http://products.office.com/en-us/visio/flowchart-software) to generate a wave generator board from wooden scrap material. This let me experiment with the Microsoft Garage's [Epilog Helix](https://www.epiloglaser.com/laser-machines/product-line/) laser cutter.

This board was designed to recreate the [standing wave](http://hyperphysics.phy-astr.gsu.edu/hbase/Waves/standw.html) experiment with a linear actuator being driven with a [Fourier Transform](https://www.mathworks.com/help/signal/ug/discrete-fourier-transform.html)-generated waveform.
