While experimenting with Autodesk 123D Make (alternate download [link](https://autodesk-123d-make.en.lo4d.com/windows)), I discovered simple support for slicing models to generate laser-cut plans. This functionality makes it really, really easy to take a 3D model and generate plans for a physical realization of those plans.

With this program, I converted the [Space Shuttle Model](http://nasa3d.arc.nasa.gov/detail/sts) from [NASA](http://www.nasa.gov/) to a series of plans, which I then laser-cut.
