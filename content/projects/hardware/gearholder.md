## About

Available from [Thingiverse](http://www.thingiverse.com/thing:11071), this model cannot be produced with traditional manufacturing techniques. The wooden gears (spares for the printer) cannot physically fit through the holes in the model.

This print was performed at thie highest resolution my printer can handle, 0.1 mm.

Print time was 3 hours.
