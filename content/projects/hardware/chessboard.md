## About

With access to either an [Epilog Helix](https://www.epiloglaser.com/laser-machines/product-line/) or a [Universal](http://www.ulsinc.com/products/) laser, you can make your own chess board.

![Laser-engraved chess board with marbled squares.](/projects/hardware-projects/laser-cut/chess-board/board.jpg "Laser-engraved chess board")
![Laser-engraved chess board with pieces](/projects/hardware-projects/laser-cut/chess-board/boardWithPieces.jpg "Chess board with pieces")

This chess board was created within 10 minutes using [Visio](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Visio), and engraved in 30 minutes on the Universal laser.

Normally the translucent (engraved) squares would be completely opaque, but by using a reduced engraving power I instead engraved marbled squares.

With 3"-scale chess pieces from the [local game store](http://www.unclesgames.com/), the chess set was made complete.
