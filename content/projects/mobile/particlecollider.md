**iPAQ PDA Particle Collider** was a small mobile application to simulate particle orbits around gravity/electromagnetic sources and sinks.

![Particles without traces](/projects/mobile-projects/pda/particle-collider/particleCollider1.png "Particles No Traces")

The main focus of this program was to see how effectively the PDA could compute particle orbits while rendering them simultaneously.

![Particles with traces](/projects/mobile-projects/pda/particle-collider/particleCollider2.png "Particles With Traces")
