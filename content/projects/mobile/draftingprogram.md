## About

This simple application takes the top and front view of drafting lines to construct a 3D wireframe view of the lines.

![An empty drafting pane](/projects/mobile-projects/pda/drafting-program/perspectiveDrafting1.png "Empty Drafting Pane")

I wrote this program to simplify drafting homework

> This program verifies proper 3D perspection of 2D lines.
