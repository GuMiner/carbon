## About

**Beam Flow** is an educational simulation-based presentation software, which I wrote for the UW - Madison NEST competition.

This program was a larger success than I thought -- I placed in the 3rd annual UW-Madison CS [competition](https://contest.cs.wisc.edu/) (better archive [link](/projects/simulation-projects/beam-flow/BeamFlowWinners.pdf)).
