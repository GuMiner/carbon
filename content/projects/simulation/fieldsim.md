## About

**Field Simulator** simulates simple static electric and gravitational fields, graphing the results in 2D and 3D.

![Three-particle 2D electric field diagram](/projects/simulation-projects/field-sim/fieldSimulator1.png "Three-particle 2D electric field diagram")

![Three-particle 3D electric field diagram](/projects/simulation-projects/field-sim/fieldSimulator2.png "Three-particle 3D electric field diagram")

This program was initially written to simplify rote calculations for physics homework.

Visualizations were later added for debugging, validation, and experimentation.
